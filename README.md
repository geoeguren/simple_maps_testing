# simple maps
### Making maps simple with AI.

A conversational interface for generating maps. Type what you want to see, and the AI builds it. This MVP covers Argentina — more countries coming.

---

## Layers

**Connected**
- ✅ Provinces
- ✅ Departments
- ✅ Localities
- ✅ National road network
- ✅ Protected areas
- ✅ International border crossings
- ✅ Ports
- ✅ Antarctic bases
- ✅ Bridges

**Coming soon**

- ❌ Railways

- ❌ Elevation / topography
- ❌ Satellite imagery overlay
- ❌ Schools and hospitals
- ❌ Electoral districts

Data source: [Instituto Geográfico Nacional](https://ign.gob.ar)
